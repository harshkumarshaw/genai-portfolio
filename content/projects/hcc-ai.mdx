## Overview

HCC-AI is an end-to-end, research-grade pipeline aimed at accelerating drug discovery for Liver Cancer (Hepatocellular Carcinoma). It integrates state-of-the-art deep learning and bioinformatics to systematically discover and validate drug candidates.

## Problem

Traditional drug discovery is prohibitively slow and expensive. Rare and under-resourced diseases require accelerated pipelines that leverage modern deep learning (like AlphaFold3 and generative models) to rapidly simulate and validate targets before wet-lab testing.

## Why This Approach ⭐

Drug discovery cannot be solved by a single model. We architected a **6-phase structured pipeline** that cascades data from raw genetic mutations all the way to 3D protein simulation. This modular approach ensures that each step is biologically grounded and scientifically verifiable.

## Solution + Architecture

The pipeline consists of six distinct phases:
1. **Subtype-GAN**: Disease subtype stratification using Generative Adversarial Networks.
2. **Biomarker Scoring**: Using the AlphaMissense API to score mutation impacts.
3. **MMDRP**: Multi-Modal Drug Response Prediction.
4. **Drug Repurposing**: Utilizing PyKEEN + BioGPT for knowledge graph-based repurposing.
5. **Protein Simulation**: AlphaFold3 Server for structural prediction.
6. **Digital Twin**: Final validation.

**Compute Strategy:** 
We engineered a highly resource-aware compute orchestration: 
- **Local RTX 3050 (4GB)** for rapid prototyping and lightweight training.
- **Kaggle P100** for heavy inference.
- **Google Colab T4** for auxiliary tasks.
- **AlphaFold Server** free tier for complex structure prediction.

## Results / Impact

- The pipeline's top-20 candidates successfully matched known, clinically approved drugs (e.g., Sorafenib, Lenvatinib), proving the system's validity.
- Achieved **end-to-end pipeline execution in under 8 weeks**.

## Key Insights ⭐

- **Hardware-aware engineering is essential:** Effectively distributing the workload across free-tier cloud GPUs and local hardware allowed for enterprise-scale research on a zero-dollar compute budget.

## System Thinking Snapshot ⭐

The pipeline demonstrates a profound understanding of data hand-offs. The output of the GAN directly informs the BioGPT prompts, whose outputs are then structurally validated by AlphaFold, creating a tightly coupled, automated scientific workflow.
